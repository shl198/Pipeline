The files generated by DetectConamination are:
==============================================

1.read files:
-------------
	*fastqfiles filename

2.After mapping using gsnap:
----------------------------
	* filename.sam

3.sam to sort bam:
------------------
	* filename.sort.bam

4.extract bam containing mapped reads:
--------------------------------------
	* filename.mapped.sort.bam

5.bam to sam:
-------------
	* filename.mapped.sort.sam

6.get stats for sam file:
-------------------------
	* filename.mapped.sort.sam.uniq_seq.fa  (this file is output of uniq sequence mapping to microbiome)
	* filename.mapped.sort.txt (stores the # of reads, # of unique read, # of unique sequence mapping to microbiome)

7.view mapping result in IGV:(no files)
-----------------------------

8.blastn unique sequence:
------------------------
	* filename.mapped.sort.sam.uniq_seq.blast.txt
